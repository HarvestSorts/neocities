Preserving your own history is important!

08/20/2025

PRESERVE YOUR HISTORY, PEOPLE!!!

Ever since 2020, I was always peculiar about how I handle my history. Previous chats, screenshots, past Minecraft worlds... I just want to preserve all of it. I care a lot about how I archived my things, because you never know if it'll all up and vanish one day! I've had so many Discord accounts that I've gone through, some accounts just straight up deleted because I was super paranoid, and Discord Chat Exporter has since become a very important part of my personal arsenal of tools. It's so important!!!

I just love having a personal library of stuff I can browse, because I can just go through everything on my own time. With my drive dying in June, I can't really do that easily anymore, but I've tried my best to back everything up that I want the most, so hopefully things stay smooth sailing!
